What are the symptoms of Monkeypox?

The early symptoms are fever, headache, muscle aches, backache, chills and exhaustion.

After 1-3 days of the fever, a rash may form that initially looks like pimples, but changes into almost a blister. This rash can be found on the face, inside the mouth, and on other parts of the body.

The rash kind of happens all at the same time, and lasts until the scab falls off.
